Privacy Policy

We take data stewardship seriously. This overview explains how SpamShield treats the messages you submit.

How We Process Messages

Messages submitted through the web interface are processed in-memory and never written to disk. Once the prediction response is returned, the raw text is discarded.

Internal Processing

Risk scoring is performed entirely within the SpamShield service. No external scoring providers or third-party endpoints receive message content.

Logging

Application logs capture only truncated message previews (first 50 characters) to assist with debugging. You can disable this behavior by adjusting the logging statements in app.py.

Contact

If you have compliance questions, raise an issue in the repository or reach out to your platform administrator for assistance.
